# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 15, 2026  
**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026

Summit Staffing Pty Ltd ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose personal information in connection with the Summit Staffing mobile application ("the App") in accordance with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users.

### 1.1 Personal Information
- **Account Data:** Email address, name, phone number, and password.
- **Profile Data:** Profile pictures, service types, biographies, and ratings.
- **Verification Documents:** For support workers, we collect and store training certificates, NDIS worker screening results, and other identification documents to ensure compliance.

### 1.2 Financial Information
- **Payments:** Payment processing is handled by Stripe. We do not store full credit card details on our servers. We collect information related to invoices, payment history, and worker earnings.

### 1.3 Communication Data
- **Messages:** We collect and store contents of messages sent between participants and support workers via the App's messaging system to facilitate service delivery and dispute resolution.

### 1.4 Device and Usage Data
- **Technical Information:** IP address, device type, operating system version, and App usage statistics.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- **Service Delivery:** To connect NDIS participants with support workers.
- **Compliance:** To verify worker credentials and satisfy NDIS requirements.
- **Communications:** To send notifications regarding bookings, messages, and account updates.
- **Payments:** To facilitate secure transactions and payouts via Stripe.
- **Support:** To provide customer assistance and resolve disputes.

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only in the following circumstances:
- **Between Users:** Limited profile information is shared between participants and workers to facilitate bookings.
- **Service Providers:** With third-party partners like Stripe (payments), AWS (storage), and Mailgun/Twilio (communications).
- **Legal Requirements:** When required by Australian law or to protect our legal rights.

## 4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:
- Encryption of data in transit and at rest.
- Secure document storage for sensitive verification files.
- Restrictive access controls for internal administrators.

## 5. Your Rights and Access

You have the right to:
- Access the personal information we hold about you.
- Request corrections to inaccurate data.
- Request the deletion of your account and associated data (subject to legal retention requirements).
