## Full Description
**Summit Staffing – Professional NDIS Support Connections**

Summit Staffing facilitates seamless connections between NDIS participants and qualified, verified support workers. Built specifically for the Australian NDIS landscape, our platform ensures safety, transparency, and ease of use for all users.

**For Participants:**
- **Find the Right Fit:** Browse verified support worker profiles, read reviews, and view qualifications.
- **Easy Bookings:** Schedule shifts that fit your life with just a few taps.
- **Direct Messaging:** Communicate securely with your support team in real-time.
- **Transparent Invoicing:** Access and manage all your invoices and payment history in one place.

**For Support Workers:**
- **Manage Your Career:** Set your availability and accept shifts that suit your schedule.
- **Compliance Made Simple:** Upload and manage your NDIS verification documents securely.
- **Track Earnings:** View your completed shifts and upcoming payments with professional dashboards.
- **Integrated Comms:** Stay in touch with your clients through our dedicated messaging system.

**Key Features:**
- Secure document verification for NDIS compliance.
- Real-time shift notifications.
- Integrated payment processing via Stripe.
- Professional profile management.
- Comprehensive help and support.

Join Summit Staffing today and experience a better way to manage NDIS support services.
