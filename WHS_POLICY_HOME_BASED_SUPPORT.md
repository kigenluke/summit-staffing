# Work Health and Safety (WHS) Policy: Home-Based Support Services

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** April 13, 2026  
**Company:** Summit Staffing Pty Ltd

## 1. Purpose
This policy sets minimum safety expectations for workers, vendors, and participants when services are delivered in homes or community settings.

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to:
- Independent support workers
- Vendor personnel providing transport, equipment, therapeutic, or home/community services
- Summit Staffing platform users involved in bookings

## 3. Core Safety Requirements
- Follow lawful and safe work practices at all times.
- Do not perform tasks outside your training, qualification, or authorization.
- Use appropriate PPE and safe manual handling techniques where required.
- Immediately stop work and escalate where serious risk is identified.

## 4. Pre-Shift Risk Checks
Before starting a booking, workers/vendors should confirm:
- Service address and known hazards
- Access requirements (stairs, lifts, pets, mobility equipment)
- Any participant-specific risk controls
- Emergency contact details

## 5. Incident Reporting and Escalation
- All incidents, near misses, injuries, or unsafe situations must be reported as soon as possible.
- Critical incidents must be escalated immediately through Summit Staffing support channels.
- Where required, Summit Staffing may notify relevant authorities and regulators.

## 6. Manual Handling and High-Risk Tasks
- Workers must use approved manual handling techniques.
- Mechanical aids should be used where available and appropriate.
- Unsafe lifting or transfer requests must be declined and escalated.

## 7. Violence, Harassment, and Unsafe Behaviour
- Violence, abuse, threats, or harassment are not tolerated.
- Workers may leave unsafe environments and report the event immediately.
- Summit Staffing may suspend involved accounts pending investigation.

## 8. Training and Compliance
- Workers/vendors are responsible for maintaining valid credentials and mandatory safety training.
- Summit Staffing may request updated evidence for ongoing platform access.

## 9. Review
This policy will be reviewed periodically to align with NDIS requirements and applicable WHS law.
